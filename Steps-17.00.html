The SCRIPTS (This applies to ALL FOUR combo charts).  The below scripts follow the same order as above and how it was developed.  

NOTE:  Numbers 1-5 are the LEFT side of the tooltip so they are not numbers therefore do not enable "Format as number" after adding the script in the textbox as shown below.
  

1. Total Sales  ='Sales: ' &subfield(vl_viztipsSet,chr(39),2)
2. Sales YoY% ='Sales YoY%: ' &subfield(vl_viztipsSet,chr(39),2)
3. Unit YoY%  ='Unit YoY%: ' &subfield(vl_viztipsSet,chr(39),2)
4. Price YoY%  ='Price YoY%: ' &subfield(vl_viztipsSet,chr(39),2)
5. Market Share  ='Mkt Share: ' &subfield(vl_viztipsSet,chr(39),2)

NOTE:  Numbers 6-10 are the RIGHT side of the tooltip so they are corresponding numbers to the first five layers therefore enable "Format as number" after adding the script in the textbox as shown below.

6. Total Sales # =Sum($(vl_viztipsSet)[Units Sold] * [Price per Unit])

7. # Sale YoY%%  =((count({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2021 <=12/31/2021"}>} ([Price per Unit] * [Units Sold])) - count({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2020 <=12/31/2020"}>} ([Price per Unit] * [Units Sold]))) / Avg({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2020 <=12/31/2021"}$(vl_viztipsInlineSet)>} ([Price per Unit] * [Units Sold]))) / 20

8. # Price YoY% =((Sum({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2020 <=12/31/2020"}>} [Price per Unit]) - Sum({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2021 <=12/31/2021"}>} [Price per Unit]))) / Avg({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2021 <=12/31/2020"}$(vl_viztipsInlineSet)>} [Price per Unit])  / 600000

9. # Unit YoY% =((Count({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2021 <=12/31/2021"}>} [Units Sold])
- Count({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2020 <=12/31/2020"}>} [Units Sold])) / Sum({<YearMonth={">=01/01/2020 <=12/31/2021"}$(vl_viztipsInlineSet)>} [Units Sold]))  * 50

10. # Market Share =SUM({<InvoiceMonth, Region, Season, Color, Brand, Gender>}[Units Sold]) /
SUM({<InvoiceMonth, Region, Season, Color, Brand, Gender $(vl_viztipsInlineSet)>}[Units Sold]) / 50
