Dimension Field and Measure expression scripts:

Dimension:  "Color"

Measure 1 (Market Share):  SUM(TOTAL <Color> [Units Sold]) / SUM(TOTAL <InvoiceMonth, Region, Season, Brand, Gender> [Units Sold])

Measure 2 (Price YoY%):     ((Avg({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(Max(YearMonth)))) <=$(=Max(YearMonth))"}>} [Price per Unit]) - Avg({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(AddYears(Max(YearMonth),-1)))) <=$(=Num(AddYears(Max(YearMonth),-1)))"}>} [Price per Unit])) / Avg({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(Max(YearMonth)))) <=$(=Max(YearMonth))"}>} [Price per Unit])) 

Measure 3 (Units YoY%):    ((Avg({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(Max(InvoiceDate)))) <=$(=Max(InvoiceDate))"}>} [Units Sold]) - Avg({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(AddYears(Max(InvoiceDate),-1)))) <=$(=Num(AddYears(Max(InvoiceDate),-1)))"}>} [Units Sold])) / Avg({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(AddYears(Max(InvoiceDate),-1)))) <=$(=Num(AddYears(Max(InvoiceDate),-1)))"}>} [Units Sold]))                 

Measure 4 (Sales YoY%):  ((Sum({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(Max(YearMonth)))) <=$(=Max(YearMonth=))"}>} ([Price per Unit] * [Units Sold])) - Sum({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(AddYears(Max(YearMonth),-1)))) <=$(=Num(AddYears(Max(YearMonth),-1)))"}>} ([Price per Unit] * [Units Sold]))) / Sum({<Color=, YearMonth={">=$(=Num(YearStart(AddYears(Max(YearMonth),-1)))) <=$(=Num(AddYears(Max(YearMonth),-1)))"}>} ([Price per Unit] * [Units Sold]))) / 5
